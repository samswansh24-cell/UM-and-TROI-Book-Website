About the Author

BriBooks Author
Published Age 11 & 12

"Creativity doesn't wait for permission — it just needs a spark."

Reyansh Sethi is a Class VII student who has already accomplished what many adults dream of — authoring two published books before the age of thirteen. With an instinct for storytelling that belies his years, he crafts narratives that feel both magical and deeply relatable.

His debut Unlock Imagination introduced readers to a world where ideas have power. His follow-up The Return of Imagination expanded that universe with greater ambition and narrative depth — proving that his debut was no beginner's luck.

When he isn't writing, Reyansh is a curious student exploring the world with the same wonder that fills his pages.
